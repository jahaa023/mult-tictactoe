Personal info

You can change personal info like your password and e-mail adress in the settings too. Note that to change this info, you need access to the email adress you created the account with to prevent hacking.

In order to change your password or e-mail, head to the "Personal info" tab in the settings. From there you can input your new password or email adress and you will receive a code on your email. Input the code and your info gets changed.
