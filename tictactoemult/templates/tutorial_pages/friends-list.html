Friends list

You can also add friends in Tic Tac Toe, and invite them to matches.

To do this, head to the "Friends page".

Adding friend

To add friends, head over to the "Add friends" tab and search for an accounts username or nickname.

To send a friend request, click the person icon with a plus next to the users username. You will get a popup on the top of the screen confirming the request has been sent.

You can view all your outgoing and incoming friend requests in the "Pending invites" tab. Here you can cancel outgoing friend requests, as well as accept or ignore incoming ones.

If you have an incoming invite, a red bubble will pop up on the friends button.

You can decline or accept an incoming invite in the pending invites tab.

You can view all your current friends in the "Your friends" tab. You can see if they're offline or online.

Removing a friend

To remove a friend, head to the Your friends tab and put your mouse over their username. Then click the three dots to the right. Then press "Remove friend".
