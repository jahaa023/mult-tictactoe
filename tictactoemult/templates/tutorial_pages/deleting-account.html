Deleting account

If you wish to delete your account, head to the settings and click the red text that says "Delete account".

After you press "Delete account" on the popup, you will receive a code on the email the account is registered to.

Input the code and press "Confirm". This will then delete your account.

Warning: if you delete your account, it will be removed from:

  • Your friends friend list
  • The leaderboard

There is no way to recover the account after its been deleted.
