Editing profile

There are a number of things you can change in your profile, so lets go through them!

Changing profile picture

In order to change your profile picture, head to the Settings and click on the profile picture.

A popup will appear where you will need to upload an image of your choosing.

You can then crop the image to your liking and press save. Your profile picture and banner color then changes.

Editing nickname and description

In order to change your nickname and description, simply click on them, type in your new nickname or description and click the "Save changes" button.
